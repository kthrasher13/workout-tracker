Hamstrings

Warm up (2 sets each)

  • 10-15 Walking lunges
  • 10-15 Leg swings
  • 10-15 High knees
  • 30s jog in place

Week 1

  • Day 1
    • Hamstring kicks (1 set 15 reps)

      • Stand with your feet hip-width apart and extend one arm in front of you. Swing that leg up in front of you and back down while keeping your leg as straight as possible and your toes pointed up. Repeat with the other leg.

    • Single Leg RDL (1 set 15 reps)

      • Stand with your feet hip-width apart, shift your weight onto one leg and slightly bend the other. Keep your back straight while hinging at your hips. Use your hamstring to pull yourself back up.

    • Toe Touch (2 sets 30s)

      • Stand with your feet closer together. Hinge at your hips and lower your upper body towards your toes. Reach your hands towards your feet, try to touch your toes, the ground, or as far down your legs as you can comfortably go.

  • Day 2
    • Elephant walks (1 set 30 reps)

      • Hinge at your hips until you feel a stretch behind your knees. Bend one leg while the other is straight then fluidly bend the other knee while straightening the first knee.

    • Good Morning (1 set 10 reps)

      • Stand with your feet more than shoulder-width apart (wider stance) and place your hands on your hips. Hinge at your hips and lower your upper body towards the ground while keeping your back straight then come back up.

    • Single Leg Hamstring Stretch (2 sets 30s)

      • Sit on the ground with one leg straight out in front of you and the other leg bent in toward you. Reach forward with both hands, trying to touch the toes of your straight leg.

  • Day 3
    • Hamstring kicks (1 set 15 reps)

      • Stand with your feet hip-width apart and extend one arm in front of you. Swing that leg up in front of you and back down while keeping your leg as straight as possible and your toes pointed up. Repeat with the other leg.

    • Single Leg RDL (1 set 10 reps)

      • Stand with your feet hip-width apart, shift your weight onto one leg and slightly bend the other. Keep your back straight while hinging at your hips. Use your hamstring to pull yourself back up.

    • Toe Touch (2 sets 30s)

      • Stand with your feet closer together. Hinge at your hips and lower your upper body towards your toes. Reach your hands towards your feet, try to touch your toes, the ground, or as far down your legs as you can comfortably go.

Week 2

  • Day 1
    • Elephant walks (1 set 30 reps)

      • Hinge at your hips until you feel a stretch behind your knees. Bend one leg while the other is straight then fluidly bend the other knee while straightening the first knee.

    • Good morning (1 set 10 reps)

      • Stand with your feet more than shoulder-width apart (wider stance) and place your hands on your hips. Hinge at your hips and lower your upper body towards the ground while keeping your back straight then come back up.

    • Single Leg Hamstring Stretch (2 sets 30s)

      • Sit on the ground with one leg straight out in front of you and the other leg bent in toward you. Reach forward with both hands, trying to touch the toes of your straight leg.

  • Day 2
    • Sit and reach (1 set 10 reps)

      • Sit on the ground with your legs extended straight in front of you. Reach both hands forward, trying to reach past your toes or as far as you can go. Return to the starting position and repeat.

    • Sit and reach (1 set 30s)

      • Sit on the ground with your legs extended straight in front of you. Reach both hands forward, trying to reach past your toes or as far as you can go. Hold this position to feel the stretch.

    • Flat back hamstring stretch (2 sets 30s)

      • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and place your hands on your hips. Hinge at your hips and lower your upper body towards the ground while keeping your back and legs straight.

  • Day 3
    • Crossbody leg swings (1 set 15 reps)

      • Hold onto a wall or something near you for support. Swing one leg across the front of your body, then back and out to the side. Repeat while slowly increasing your range of motion.

    • Standing pancake (1 set 30s)

      • Stand and open your legs as wide as possible into a straddle position. Then bend at the hips , pushing them back while keeping your back straight.

    • Pancake (2 sets 30s)

      • Sit on the ground and open your legs as wide as possible into a straddle position. Lean forward at your hips, trying to reach as far as you can in front of you. Keep your back straight and chest open

  • Day 3
    • Standing pancake good morning (1 set 10 reps)

      • Stand with your feet wider than shoulder-width apart and toes pointed slightly outward. Hinge at your hips and lower your upper body towards the ground, keeping your back straight and chest open

    • Hamstring chokes (1 set 15 reps)

      • Sit on the ground and bend one leg, bringing your knee toward you. Place your hand behind your hamstring and gently pull your leg toward your chest, feeling a stretch in the back of your thigh. Straighten your leg, extedning up toward the ceiling

Week 4

      • Stand with your feet hip-width apart and slowly roll your upper body forward, one vertebra at a time, until your hands touch the ground or as far as you can comfortably go. Roll back up to the starting position in the same controlled manner.
